2️⃣ Formula cashout (semplificata)
Caso: BACK piazzato, cashout = LAY
Copia codice
Text
profit_if_win  = (odds_back - 1) * stake
liability_lay  = (odds_lay - 1) * stake_lay

stake_lay = (odds_back * stake) / odds_lay
Profitto netto (con commissione):
Copia codice
Text
profit = (stake_lay - stake) * (1 - commission)
📌 In pratica:
quota LAY ↓ → cashout ↑
quota LAY ↑ → cashout ↓
